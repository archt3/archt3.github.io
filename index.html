Metamorphic Buffers for Multijunction Solar Cells

My group researched and presented on metamorhpic bufffers for use in multijunctional solar cells. In more detail: how to design solar cells to be more efficient by using metamorphic buffers to conjoin solar cell material with different atomic sizes which are all responsive to various light frequency ranges. The goal was to cover as much of the electromagnetic spectrum as possible (thereby increasing efficiency) and using these metamorphic buffers was a way to do so.
